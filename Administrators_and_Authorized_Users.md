# Administrators and Authorized Users

## Admin Definitions
Administrators are responsible for overseeing the system and ensuring its proper functioning. They have advanced privileges that allow them to perform critical tasks beyond regular users.

## Key Rules for Account Owners and Authorized Users
1. **Responsibility**: Account owners must ensure that their accounts are secure and not shared with unauthorized individuals.
2. **Access Control**: Only authorized users should be granted access to sensitive information and administrative functions.
3. **Compliance**: All users must adhere to the organization's policies and procedures when using their accounts.

## Privileges and Permissions
- **Administrators**:
  - Full access to all system features.
  - Ability to create, modify, or delete user accounts.
  - Permission to manage system settings and configurations.

- **Authorized Users**:
  - Access to specific features as defined by their role.
  - Ability to view and edit content relevant to their responsibilities.
  - Limited administrative capabilities, such as managing their own profiles.

For further clarification on roles and responsibilities, refer to the official guidelines available in the documentation.
