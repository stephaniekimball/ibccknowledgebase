# Caller Validation

## Call Greeting
"Thank you for calling [Company Name]. How can I assist you today?"

## Call Closing
"Thank you for calling [Company Name]. Have a great day!"

## Core Validation Rules
1. Verify the caller's identity using their account number and personal information.
2. Confirm the reason for the call matches their account activities.
3. Ensure all information provided is up to date.

## Inbound/Outbound Validation Requirements
### For Account Owners:
- Must provide account number and verifying information (e.g., last four digits of Social Security Number).
- Confirm identity via securely provided data.

### For Authorized Users:
- Must identify themselves and the account owner they represent.
- Must provide their own authentication information.

## Failed Verification Procedures
1. If the caller fails to verify their identity:
   - Notify them of the failure and attempt to provide alternatives.
   - Offer to transfer to a supervisor if applicable.
2. Log the failed attempts for security auditing purposes.

## Internal Transfers
- Follow internal protocol for transferring calls to different departments.
- Ensure the caller's information and reason for call are clearly communicated to the next representative.

## Validation Steps
1. Begin with basic information verification.
2. Follow up with additional security questions if needed.
3. Document the verification process in the system.

## Third Party Caller Handling
- Confirm authorization from the account owner before discussing account details.
- Document all communications with third-party callers and notify the account owner of the inquiry.
