# Account Tags and Statuses

## Account Status Layers

1. **Layer 1: New Account**
   - Description: Accounts that have just been created and not yet activated.
  
2. **Layer 2: Active Account**
   - Description: Fully operational accounts that are in use by customers.

3. **Layer 3: Suspended Account**
   - Description: Accounts that have been temporarily deactivated due to non-compliance or other issues.

## Status Tag Matrix

| Status         | Tag            | Description                               |
|----------------|----------------|-------------------------------------------|
| New            | NEW            | Newly created accounts                     |
| Active         | ACTIVE         | Accounts currently in good standing       |
| Suspended      | SUSPENDED      | Accounts that require action to reinstate  |

## Tier-1 Agent Procedures

1. **For New Accounts**:
   - Verify email addresses.
   - Send activation link.
  
2. **For Active Accounts**:
   - Monitor account activity.
   - Address customer queries promptly.
  
3. **For Suspended Accounts**:
   - Notify the customer of suspension.
   - Provide steps to resolve issues.
