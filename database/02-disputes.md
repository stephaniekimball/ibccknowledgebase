# Transaction Dispute Intake Procedures

## Eligibility Requirements
1. **Transaction Type**: Must be eligible for a dispute as defined by the organization.
2. **Timeframe**: Disputes must be initiated within a certain timeframe post-transaction (e.g., 60 days).
3. **Proof of Transaction**: Submit necessary documentation proving the transaction.
4. **Account Status**: The account should be in good standing without any restrictions.

## Channels for Dispute Intake
- **Admin Portal**: Access the admin portal to initiate a dispute through a secure form.
- **Mobile App**: Utilize the designated feature in the mobile application for disputes.

## Dispute Intake Steps in Admin Portal
1. **Log In**: Access the admin portal with your credentials.
2. **Navigate to Disputes Section**: Go to the dispute section found in the dashboard.
3. **Fill Out Form**: Complete the necessary details including transaction ID and reason for dispute.
4. **Upload Documents**: Provide any required supporting documents.
5. **Submit Dispute**: Review your information and submit the dispute request.

## Dispute via Mobile App
1. **Open App**: Launch the mobile application on your device.
2. **Access Dispute Feature**: Navigate to the disputes section within the app.
3. **Enter Transaction Details**: Fill in details like transaction ID and reason for dispute.
4. **Attach Documentation**: Upload any relevant documents as needed.
5. **Submit**: Confirm and submit your dispute.

## Important Notes
- **Provisional Credits**: Be aware that provisional credits may be issued while your dispute is being reviewed. This is typically done to ease the financial burden while your case is processed.
- **Dispute Certification**: Make sure to certify your dispute by confirming the accuracy of the information provided; false claims may result in penalties.

## Conclusion
Following the outlined steps and ensuring eligibility will help streamline the dispute process effectively.
